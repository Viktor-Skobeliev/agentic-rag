# Remote Work Policy

Northwind is a remote-first company. Employees may work from anywhere within a
timezone that overlaps the core hours of 11:00 to 15:00 UTC.

## Core hours

Everyone should be reachable during core hours, 11:00 to 15:00 UTC, for meetings
and quick questions. Outside core hours you manage your own schedule.

## Equipment

Each employee receives a company laptop and a one-time home-office budget of
1000 USD for a desk, chair and monitor. The budget is claimed through the
expenses system within the first 60 days.

## Coworking

If you prefer an office, Northwind reimburses a coworking membership up to
200 USD per month against a receipt.
