# Onboarding

## First week

On day one you receive accounts for email, chat and the code repository. Your
manager assigns an onboarding buddy who answers day-to-day questions during your
first month.

## Probation

New employees have a probation period of 3 months. Feedback is shared at the end
of the first and third months.

## Time off

Employees accrue 20 paid vacation days per year. Vacation requests go through the
HR portal and need manager approval at least 5 working days in advance.
