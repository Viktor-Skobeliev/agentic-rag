# Expenses and Reimbursement

## Submitting expenses

Submit expenses through the finance portal with a receipt attached. Claims under
100 USD are approved automatically; larger claims need manager approval.

## Reimbursement schedule

Approved expenses are paid out with the next monthly payroll run, on the last
working day of the month.

## Travel

Book travel through the company travel desk. Economy flights and mid-range hotels
are covered. A daily meal allowance of 50 USD applies while travelling.
